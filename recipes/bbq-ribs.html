Smoked Baby Back BBQ Ribs

The Best Smoked BBQ Ribs You've Ever Tasted

Here is the real smoked ribs recipe, good enough to bring home a trophy in a cook-off.

They come off the grill tender yet pleasingly chewy, with just the right hit of smoky goodness.

Ingredients:

  • 1 slab ribs
  • 4 tablespoons of Meathead's Memphis Dust
  • 1 teaspoon of Kosher Salt
  • 6 tablespoons of Sweet Baby Ray's barbecue sauce
